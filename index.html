üìä Women's Safety in India

üî¥ **Violence Against Women:**
Over 4 lakh cases of crimes against women were reported in 2019, including rape, dowry deaths, domestic violence, and harassment.

üî¥ **Sexual Assault:**
In 2019, more than 32,000 cases of rape were reported. Many more cases go unreported due to **social stigma** and **fear of reprisal**.

üî¥ **Domestic Violence:**
A national survey found that **one-third of women** in India have experienced physical or sexual violence by their intimate partners.

üî¥ **Acid Attacks:**
India has one of the highest rates of **acid attacks**, causing **severe physical and psychological trauma** for victims.

üî¥ **Child Marriage:**
Around **27% of girls** in India are married before the age of **18**, exposing them to greater risks of violence and limited opportunities.

üî¥ **Human Trafficking:**
India is a hotspot for human trafficking, with women and girls being exploited for **forced labor, sexual exploitation, and domestic servitude**.
